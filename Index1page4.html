Beginner's guide to sonic experimentation

The use of a guitar slide can give your guitar a unique glissandro effect and create deep vibratos. Slides made of different materials such as glass or metal give off different tones. The use of a slide is most popular in blues music but it has also appeared in avant-garde movements such as No wave
